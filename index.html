Career Guidance

We use personality assessments to help guide students with their career choices. Help students build a strong foundation through interview skills and internships. Identify fastest growing careers within their areas of interest. Determine Colleges with the best fit for career paths and personal interests.

Tutoring

We tutor over 40 subjects and 7 foreign languages; i.e., ASL, Chinese, English, French, German, Spanish, and Russian. Tutoring services are available for students in grades K-12. Our tutors all reside within the United States and have earned a minimum of a bachelor’s degree. We provide unlimited tutoring 24/7/365 days a year. Our tutors have passed a federal background check. Tutors are matched to students based on personality assessments, learning styles, and subject areas. Prior to being placed with students, tutors complete personal assessments and are required to pass tests in their area of expertise.

Social and Emotional Counseling

Behavioral Assessments are conducted, and counselors observe and assess student interactions with peers. Counselors are all certified as LCSWs and/or behavioral psychologists. We support students who have school-administered IEPs.

Essay Support

Tutors review and provide feedback on student-written essays for college admissions and/or scholarship applications. Essays are very important to inform colleges who you are, what you are about, and why they would want to select you. We help make sure you are presenting your best self to help your application stand out.
